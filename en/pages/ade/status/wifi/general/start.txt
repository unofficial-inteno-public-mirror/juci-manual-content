# WiFi Status

The **general WiFI Status** view displays information about your wireless channels and network interfaces.

## Configuration

For each [wireless radio](/glossary/w/wireless_radio)information is displayed about:

* [WiFi channel](/glossary/w/wifi_channel) in use.

* [Noise level](/glossary/n/noise_level) in dB for the channel.

* [WiFi interface](/glossary/w/wifi_interface) name.

* [WiFi encryption](/glossary/w/wifi_encryption) used by the interface.

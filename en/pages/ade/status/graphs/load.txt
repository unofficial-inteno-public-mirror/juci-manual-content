# Load

The **Load** graph shows device load averages for different time recent periods.

## Graph Lines

The display is shown in realtime, and the lines represent the average over different intervals:

^ Color ^ Time ^
| Blue | 1 minute | 
| Red | 5 minutes | 
| Purple | 15 minutes |

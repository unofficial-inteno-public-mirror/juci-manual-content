# Traffic

The **Traffic** graph shows upload and download traffic for the interfaces.

## Graph Lines

Each interface is available in its own tab. The display is shown in realtime, with lines representing traffic in kbit/s:

^ Color ^ Traffic ^
| Blue | Downstream. | 
| Red | Upstream. |
